I made a simple English guide site for people working or living in Japan.

Japan Life Explained
https://japan-life-explained.netlify.app

Current guides include:
- Take-home pay and payslips
- Resident tax
- Health insurance
- Visiting a clinic
- Address registration
- Move-in costs
- Bank and phone setup loop

The goal is to explain Japanese procedures in plain English, with important Japanese words, romaji, and practical tips.
