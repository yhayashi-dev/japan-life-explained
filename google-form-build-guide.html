Google Form Build Guide

Use this page as a private checklist to create the feedback form for Japan Life Explained.

Quick answer

Create one Google Form named Japan Life Explained - Feedback and Topic Request. Use the questions below, then paste the public form link into request-topic.html later.

Recommended form settings

  • Do not collect email addresses at first.
  • Do not require sign-in.
  • Do not ask for personal information.
  • Allow one response per person only if spam becomes a problem later.
  • Show a short privacy warning at the top.
  • Keep most questions optional.

Form title

Japan Life Explained - Feedback and Topic Request

Form description

Thank you for helping improve Japan Life Explained. Please do not include private personal information such as your full address, passport number, Residence Card number, My Number, bank account number, medical details, or employer confidential information.

Questions to create

1. Which guide did you read?
Type: Short answer
Example: Resident tax, Payslip, Clinic, Move-in costs
2. Was the guide easy to understand?
Type: Multiple choice
Very easy / Mostly easy / A little confusing / Very confusing / I did not read a guide yet
3. Which part was confusing?
Type: Paragraph
Optional
4. Did the Japanese words and romaji help?
Type: Multiple choice
Yes, very helpful / Somewhat helpful / Not very helpful / I did not use them
5. What topic should be added next?
Type: Paragraph
Example: garbage rules, guarantor company, My Number card, bank account, phone contract, move-out costs
6. What is your situation?
Type: Multiple choice
Living in Japan / Planning to move to Japan / Working in Japan / Studying in Japan / Helping someone in Japan / Other
7. Are there Japanese words, forms, notices, or documents you want explained?
Type: Paragraph
Reminder: Do not include private personal information.
8. Any other feedback?
Type: Paragraph
Optional

After creating the form

  • Click Send.
  • Choose link icon.
  • Copy the public form URL.
  • Later, paste the URL into the Request a topic page.
🐱 Cat tip: Start simple. A short form that people actually answer is better than a perfect form nobody completes.
