Publish Checklist

Use this checklist after Netlify deploys are working again. The goal is to avoid uploading the ZIP and forgetting sitemap, form, and link checks.

Upload target

  • Use existing Netlify project: japan-life-explained
  • Do not create a new Netlify project.
  • Upload the newest ZIP from the project’s Deploys page.
  • Wait until the deploy says Published, not skipped.

1. Netlify deploy check

Open Deploys
Confirm the latest deploy is green and says Published.
Open the live site
https://japan-life-explained.netlify.app
Hard refresh
If the old version appears, refresh the browser or open a private window.

2. Main page checks

  • Top page opens.
  • Start here section appears.
  • Article cards open the correct articles.
  • About link opens.
  • FAQ link opens.
  • Request a topic link opens.
  • Official resources and Disclaimer open.

3. Article checks

  • Take-home pay
  • Payslip
  • Resident tax
  • Health insurance
  • Clinic
  • Address registration
  • Move-in costs
  • Guarantor company
  • Bank & phone loop

4. Feedback form check

  • Open Request a topic.
  • Click Open feedback form.
  • Confirm it opens the Google Form.
  • Submit one test response if needed.
  • Confirm the response appears in Google Forms.

5. Sitemap check

  • Open https://japan-life-explained.netlify.app/sitemap.xml.
  • Confirm the page starts with <?xml.
  • Confirm <urlset appears.
  • Confirm important article URLs are included.

6. Google Search Console

  • Open Google Search Console.
  • Select the property for https://japan-life-explained.netlify.app.
  • Go to Sitemaps.
  • Submit sitemap.xml.
  • If it says failed, wait and retry after confirming the live sitemap opens correctly.

7. Smartphone check

  • Open the site on iPhone.
  • Check top page readability.
  • Check article card spacing.
  • Check tables scroll horizontally if needed.
  • Check the Google Form link opens.

8. After publish

  • Send the Japanese review request to 1–3 people.
  • Send the English review request to someone who can read English.
  • Do not spam widely yet. First collect small feedback.
  • Fix obvious problems before sharing more broadly.
🐱 Cat tip: The first goal is not “go viral.” The first goal is “nothing embarrassing breaks.”
